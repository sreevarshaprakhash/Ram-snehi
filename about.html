Our Founder

Satguru Maharaj Ramsnei Sant Hajari Lal ji is a revered spiritual leader and saint known for his profound wisdom and devotion to divine principles. He is considered a beacon of spiritual guidance, inspiring countless individuals through his teachings and selfless service. Renowned for his humility and compassion, Satguru Ji Maharaj has dedicated his life to promoting spiritual well-being and fostering a sense of unity among people. His teachings emphasize the importance of inner peace, self-realization, and devotion to the divine through Ram naam Bhajan.

What is Bhajan?

Bhajan, a devotional practice deeply rooted in Hindu spirituality, it involves the continuous chanting of the divine name, "Ram" with every breath. This rhythmic recitation is not merely a vocal exercise but a profound journey within oneself.

The Power of Bhajan:

Through consistent bhajan for at least three hours per day, one can embark on a transformative spiritual journey. This practice is believed to activate the chakras, or energy centers in the body, leading to a heightened state of consciousness and spiritual awareness.

Connecting with the Divine:

Bhajan serves as a bridge between the individual soul and the divine.

By chanting the sacred name of Ram, practitioners create a harmonious resonance with the cosmic vibrations, fostering a sense of connection with the Supreme Being.

A Journey to Amar Lok:

With dedicated bhajan, one can aspire to attain Amar Lok, the eternal abode of the divine. This spiritual destination is characterized by infinite bliss, peace, and liberation from the cycle of birth and death.

By embracing bhajan and following the guidance of spiritual masters like Sant Hajari Lal Ji, individuals can embark on a profound spiritual quest, leading to inner peace, enlightenment, and ultimate union with the divine.
